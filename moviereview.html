Movie Review : Wolf of the wall street

The Wolf of Wall Street is a biographical drama about Jordan Belfort, a stockbroker who rises to immense wealth through corruption and fraud. It portrays his extravagant lifestyle, greed, and ultimate downfall in the world of high finance.
